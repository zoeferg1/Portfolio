Zoë Ferguson

Bio

Zoë is 17 years old, and currently lives in San Francisco with her parents, and younger sister, Olivia. She is a rising senior at the Ruth Asawa San Francisco School of the Arts (SOTA), where she is in the Musical Theatre department. In addition to Musical Theatre, Zoë is a huge women's soccer fan, and can often be found either watching or playing the sport. She is also a huge outdoors lover, and likes to backpack/explore the outdoors as much as she possibly can.

Coding Experience

Before participating in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program, Zoë had very little coding experience. She participated in one of the Girls Who Code clubs, but only for a couple weeks and barely got to scratch the surface. She is excited to have the opportunity to learn how to code alongside all the other awesome girls at GWC EA!
